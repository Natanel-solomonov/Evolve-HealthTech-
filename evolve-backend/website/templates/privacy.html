Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025 • Last Updated: June 12, 2025

Evolve Wellness Incorporated ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application, Evolve: Intelligent Wellness (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information – such as your name and phone number.
  • Health Data – metrics that you explicitly permit us to access from Apple HealthKit.
  • Device Information – details about the device and operating system you use (e.g. model, OS version).

2. How We Collect Your Data

  • Forms You Complete – for example, during account creation or profile customization.
  • Apple HealthKit Integration – only after you grant permission, we securely retrieve the health metrics necessary to personalize your experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data enables us to:

  • Personalize your wellness experience in the App.
  • Generate tailored health plans and insights.
  • Support the core functionality of the App.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We are committed to maintaining your trust, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

  • We may share your information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting (e.g., Railway), analytics, and customer support. These providers are contractually obligated to safeguard your data and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process.

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained while your account is active.
  • If your account becomes inactive, we delete your data 12 months after your last activity.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Deletion): You can request that we delete your personal data. This can be done via in-app settings or by emailing us.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to us processing your personal data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@joinevolve.app.

7. Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. All health data is processed in compliance with Apple's HealthKit policies and stored using industry-standard encryption.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children in this age group. If we learn that we have done so, we will delete the information promptly.

9. Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy and any related disputes are governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, United States.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@joinevolve.app
